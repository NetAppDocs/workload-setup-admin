= Create a custom dashboard
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ./media/

[.lead]
Create custom dashboards by describing what you want to monitor in plain language. The AI guides you through the setup so your dashboards match your work and priorities.

.Before you begin

* You need permission to access the resources you want to monitor on your dashboard.
* Set up your workloads in Workload Factory before creating dashboards to monitor them.

.About this task

Custom dashboards use an AI-guided dialog to understand what you want to visualize. You describe your monitoring needs in natural language, and the system asks clarifying questions to ensure the dashboard meets your requirements. 

Dashboard creation could take more than 3 seconds, and a progress indicator displays while your dashboard is being generated. Once created, you can refresh the dashboard to see updated data.

Remember that custom dashboards are read-only by design and cannot make changes to your storage or workload configurations.

== Create a dashboard

.Steps

. In the Workload Factory console, select *Workloads Overview* > *Custom dashboards*.
+
If this is your first dashboard, you'll see a blank screen with tips to help you get started.

. Select *Create dashboard*.

. In the AI dialog, describe what you want to monitor.
+
Describe what you want to monitor in plain language. The AI will ask a few questions to better understand your needs.
+
*Sample prompts to get you started:*
+
* *Disaster recovery monitoring*: "I am setting up a disaster recovery solution in AWS for my on-premises systems. I want to see a complete list of my on-prem resources and the status of each one at the disaster recovery site in the US East region, including whether it is configured, active, and healthy."
+
* *Multi-region capacity and cost*: "I manage storage across several cloud regions and applications. I want clear visibility into how much storage I have allocated, how much is being used, and what it costs, with details for each region and each application."
+
* *Storage protection compliance*: "I am setting up a new storage protection strategy based on my organization's standards. Show me all file systems and whether they meet these requirements:

** Database workloads: 12 daily snapshots per volume and 2 tamper-proof snapshots per week.  
** NFS and SMB shares: 7 daily snapshots per volume and 1 tamper-proof snapshot per week.  
** ARP must be enabled on all volumes.

Also add a dashboard widget that lists all volumes that do not meet these requirements."

. Respond to the AI's clarifying questions.
+
The AI may ask you to choose from several options to better understand what you need. Answer each question to help it define your dashboard.

. Review the dashboard preview and select *Create* to confirm.
+
Your answers are used to create your custom dashboard. A progress bar shows while it's being created.

. After setup finishes, the dashboard opens with the widgets you set up. It also shows a Refresh button and a “Last updated” time so you can see when the data was last loaded.

.Result

Your custom dashboard is now in the dashboard catalog. All users in your Workload Factory account can access and view it.
